## Framing insights

- The vault is not neutral intake — it is **curated in two directions**. Liquid Glass / Apple chromatic pills are captured explicitly as **specimens to refuse**, not ideas to absorb. The first three screenshots are the enemy in its own museum case. The visual training set carries a negative signal, not just positive examples.
- Nearly every pack represents the same compositional contract: **pure-black field + one dense form + one corner label strip + one accent color**. KLOROFORM, Black Flag, Cyber2k, NCL Graxebeosa, Segapunk all share this skeleton despite being different studios. The vault proves this isn't a tDR borrowing — it's a **living contemporary grammar** practiced by Enero.Studio, Massive Supply Co., Enxyclo Studio, ZULA, HVNTER, and studio KLOROFORM in 2024–2025.
- Three studios repeat: **Enero.Studio** (Black Flag, Brando Corp), **Massive Supply Co.** (Cyber2k, Diagrams2), and **NXCL/Enxyclo** (Graxebeosa) — all in the same 2023–2025 Behance/Creative Market cohort. This is the working peer-group SignalframeUX is positioning against, not historical reference.

## 1 · Shared grammar (what every pack shares)

| Element | Rule observed |
|---|---|
| **Ground** | Pure black `#000` or near-black; paper grain permitted when substrate-truth matters (Brando cream, Vanzyst purple-grain). Never soft-grey backdrop. |
| **Accent hierarchy** | **Exactly one** pop color per composition. Hi-viz lime green dominates (Black Flag, Cyber2k, NCL, Segapunk). Orange second (Cyber2k 2k, Massive Supply). Red third (Diagrams2, Brando). Iridescent/holographic as rare variant ("colored flag"). |
| **Chrome** | Corner strips: top-left = system name, top-right = studio/grid mark + date, bottom-left = catalog/studio ID, bottom-right = URL. Geo-coordinates (SOC/IDN 7°33'22"S) as parallel-world anchor. |
| **Label system** | Serialized variant IDs: `E0000_01–30`, `SYM 001–250`, `SHAPE 001–144`, `FCHMYCSF_024`. The catalog IS the brand. |
| **Type pairing** | Custom display face (expressive) + Helvetica-class utility (neutral chrome) + monospaced micro-type (technical labels). Three-voice system. |

## 2 · Form vocabulary, inventoried

**Pointcloud morphologies** (KLOROFORM, 23 images): sphere, torus, ring, comet, spiral, grid-warp, event horizon, nebula, tunnel-slice, dissolving halo. All mathematical distributions, not random scatter. **This is the SIGNAL layer's canonical vocabulary.**

**Corrugated sheet geometries** (Black Flag, 23 images): moire/halftone waving planes — roofing sheet, flag, curtain, peeling corner, wing-sail. Industrial substrate turned into a banner.

**HUD scaffolds** (Cyber2k, 10 images): octagons with clipped corners, bracket frames `[...]`, corner ticks, target reticles, +/cross accent dots, rulers, waveforms, circuit-outline frames. Direct InstrumentHUD / PROOF-section source.

**Y2K marks** (CyberForm 144, Brando Corp 250): Swiss trademark geometry — hexagons, recycling wheels, star-asterisks, nested cubes, peace-variants. Corporate-dictionary grammar re-occupied.

**Vanzyst cellular/circuit forms** (~100 shapes): pixel-compressed organic/cyber hybrids. Biology + cybernetic in one stroke-vector vocabulary. Direct machElegy anchor.

**Technical schematics** (Diagrams2, 4 images): transformers, relays, cathode/plate, frequency axes, circuit diagrams. Half-tone red+white on black paper — provides PROOF-section readout language.

## 3 · Typography — two axes of distortion

Two opposite deformations recur, never in the same piece:

| Axis | Exemplars | Use |
|---|---|---|
| **Extreme horizontal** | NCL Graxebeosa, DEN® (HVNTER) | wordmarks, hero statement lines, sample words for genre tagging ("aphex / xstacy / björk" — Warp lineage) |
| **Extreme vertical** | Segapunk (ZULA) | poster-dominant single words, condensed cathedral blackletter descendants |

Both carry **alternate glyph sets + ligatures** — the typeface is a system with 600–4800 glyphs, multilingual, variable. The font *itself* is a catalog. Every sample is labeled with spec chrome (`608 GLYPHS`, `43 UPP ALTERNATES`, debug bezier-point view).

**DEN® teaches the thesis:** the typeface shows its bezier anchor points as a debug view. The construction is the aesthetic. Process visible = substrate-as-content.

## 4 · Texture libraries (substrate-as-content anchors)

The vault holds ~40 images of pure texture reference — not compositions, just substrate:

- **Natural grids** (×4): tree bark, cracked earth, stone, moss, crystalline lattice. Industrial-romanticism material reference.
- **Acetate/plastic substrate** (Cyber2k 20 textures): black plastic wrap, crumpled film, scratched acetate.
- **Dither/pixel noise** (Vanzyst MULTICOLORED): red/blue/yellow/black pixel-erosion on paper.
- **Distress filters** (Cyber2k 5 actions): NOISE · SPECKLE · DIFFUSE · BLUR · LINE. These are the allowed distortion vocabulary for SignalframeUX SIGNAL layer.

## 6 · Anti-references (the named enemies, catalogued)

1. **Liquid Glass** (screenshots 1–3) — refractive gradient pills, chromatic bubble, iridescent fluid. These sit in the vault *as specimens to refuse*.
2. **Decorative soft gradients** without grain (batch 4 gradient packs are admitted only because they keep film-grain honesty — the clean version would fail).
3. **Rounded-corner bubble geometry** — implicit refusal across every pack (zero-radius everywhere).
4. **Apple-keynote chrome highlights + lens flare** — named in `hardish-cyberpunk.md` and visibly absent from every piece.

## 7 · Translation to SignalframeUX, concretely

- **InstrumentHUD + PROOF section:** their canonical source is Cyber2k + Diagrams2. Keep pulling from that well.
- **SIGNAL layer (shader output):** push toward KLOROFORM's point-distribution morphologies (ring / torus / comet / event-horizon). The current FBM+grid shader can add pointcloud geometries without breaking the frame contract.
- **Typography headroom:** SF does not yet have a Rudnick-tier display face. DEN® and NCL Graxebeosa show the territory — custom display, alternate glyphs, bezier-debug view. A commissioned SF display face is the missing element.
- **Corner-chrome discipline:** SF's current corner labels (`[03//PROOF]`, `SF//FRM-001`) match this lineage exactly. The bracketing is canon — don't soften.
- **Stamp vocabulary:** the green-pill stamps (`ORGULLECIDA`, `NEVER COME BACK`, `BERSERKER!`, `Nevermore`) are a missing slot. A stampable coded-mark system for CD announcements would strengthen the coded-systems layer.
- **Anti-Liquid Glass stance:** every time a collaborator reaches for a chromatic pill or refractive accent, the vault's first three screenshots are the evidence the system was designed *against* that. Use them as the negative exemplar.
