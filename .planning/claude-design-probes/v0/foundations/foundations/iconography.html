CONSTRUCTION · THE 16×16 GRID ONE PRIMITIVE · MANY GLYPHS

Grid, safe area, primitive.

Canvas
16 × 16 units. The glyph lives in the full canvas; the safe area (2 → 14) is where most blocks are placed to keep breathing room.
Primitive
SVG <rect> with rx = ry = 0.25. One primitive, composed — never a <path>, never an <ellipse>.
Fill
currentColor only. The glyph inherits from its container; no two-color icons, no gradients.
Unit block
Minimum 2 × 2 units. A 1-unit block is a rule, not a glyph.
Composition
Bars · grids · staircases · L-shapes · scatters. Compose from this vocabulary, don't invent new forms.

Rules of iconography

  • One primitive only. Rectangles on a 16×16 grid. No paths, no circles, no diagonals.
  • Corner radius is fixed. rx = 0.25 units, never more, never less.
  • Minimum block = 2×2. A 1-unit block is a rule or a dot, not a glyph element.
  • Single color. currentColor inherited from the container. No gradients, no two-tone.
  • Function over decoration. A glyph must be legible at 16px or it does not ship.
  • Compose, don't invent. Extend the catalog by composing from bars / grids / staircases / L-shapes / scatters.
