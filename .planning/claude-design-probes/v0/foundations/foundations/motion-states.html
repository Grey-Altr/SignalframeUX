The frame is static. Motion belongs to the signal — arrival, scan, ambient drift, fault pulse. Four motion kinds cover the entire system: SNAP for UI response, ARRIVE for content entry, PULSE for ambient signal, SCAN for process. Every state (loading, empty, error, success) picks one and commits.

Rules of motion & states

  • Four kinds, no more. SNAP / ARRIVE / PULSE / SCAN. If a motion doesn't fit one, it's the wrong motion.
  • Frame does not move. Layout, grid, and chrome hold still. Only signal translates.
  • Every state picks one. Loading scans. Empty holds. Error pulses once. Success arrives.
  • Durations derive from posture. Read --posture-motion-duration-*, never hand-pick ms.
  • Ease-out, never spring. cubic-bezier(0.2, 0.8, 0.4, 1) — no overshoot, no bounce.
  • Respect reduced motion. @media (prefers-reduced-motion) flattens PULSE & SCAN to static.
