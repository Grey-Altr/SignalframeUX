Rules of the signal layer

  • Carry mood, never meaning. Overlays convey no information. Any data must read with overlays at zero.
  • Stack is fixed. Grain → scanline → CRT → VHS. Reordering is a smell; a different order is a different aesthetic.
  • Always on the frame. Overlays live above content, below modal roofs. They decorate; they don't gate interaction.
  • Driven by posture. In production, overlay opacities derive from --posture-intensity. These knobs exist for tuning, not for hand-placement.
  • No shadows or borders to fake depth. Overlays simulate medium, not elevation.
  • Removable. @media (prefers-reduced-motion) kills the VHS tear. An "off" preset is always one click away.
