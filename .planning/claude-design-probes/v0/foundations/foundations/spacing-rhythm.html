Rules of spacing & rhythm

  • Nine stops, closed. Only values in --space-*. No raw px, no calc() on spacing, no eyeball-picked rem.
  • Pick a posture per region. Tight (1–3) for data. Baseline (4–8) for UI. Epic (12–24) for signal. Never interleave.
  • 8px baseline, everywhere. Component heights and vertical rhythm round to 8px. Hover the bands above to verify.
  • One epic per page. 96px gets used when something must land. If everything is epic, nothing is.
  • Density scales the stops. --posture-density multiplies control pad — the ratios stay fixed.
  • Gutter before section before control. Outer spacing must always exceed inner spacing, or the frame collapses.
